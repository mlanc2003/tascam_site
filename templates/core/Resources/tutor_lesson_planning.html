Professional Lesson Planning & Teaching Frameworks

Updated January 2026 · TASCAM Teaching Division

Effective lesson planning helps students achieve significant and measurable progress. At TASCAM Co Ltd, we follow a structured teaching framework used by leading educational organisations worldwide.

1. The TASCAM Lesson Framework

  1. Learning objectives
  2. Diagnostic assessment
  3. Guided explanation
  4. Student practice
  5. Feedback & correction
  6. Homework strategy

2. Setting SMART Learning Goals

  • Specific
  • Measurable
  • Achievable
  • Relevant
  • Time-Bound

3. Creating Long-Term Student Progress Plans

Every student should have a progress roadmap created during Week 1. This ensures structure, accountability, and measurable improvement.

4. Delivering High-Impact Online Lessons

  • Use visual aids wherever possible.
  • Check understanding at each stage.
  • Encourage active student participation.
