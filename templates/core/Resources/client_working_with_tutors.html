How to Get the Most Out of Tutoring

Updated January 2026 · TASCAM Learning Support Team

Effective tutoring is a partnership between students, parents, and tutors. This guide explains how to maximise outcomes every time your child attends a session.

1. Preparing Before Each Session

  • Bring homework, notes, and key questions.
  • Review previous feedback from your tutor.
  • Identify topics that need clarification.

2. What Happens During a Good Tutoring Session?

  • Clear learning goals at the start.
  • Interactive examples and guided practice.
  • Checking understanding through small tasks.
  • Actionable feedback at the end.

3. Parental Follow-up After the Session

  • Review progress with your child.
  • Encourage them to complete assigned practice.
  • Share updates with the tutor if needed.

4. Building a Long-Term Tutor–Student Relationship

  • Maintain consistent weekly sessions.
  • Track improvements over time.
  • Adjust learning plans as the student develops.

Strong communication between parents, students, and tutors is the key to long-term academic progress.
