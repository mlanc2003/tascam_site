How to Use the TASCAM Tutor Platform

Updated January 2026 · TASCAM Support Team

The TASCAM Tutor Platform is designed to help you manage lessons, communicate with students, upload materials, and track progress efficiently.

1. Getting Started

  • Complete your tutor profile.
  • Upload verification documents.
  • Set your availability schedule.

2. Managing Lessons

  • Accept or decline new lesson requests.
  • Use the built-in calendar for scheduling.
  • Upload lesson materials in advance.

3. Messaging Students

Messages must be:

  • Professional
  • Clear
  • Bound by academic integrity rules

4. Payment & Timesheets

  • Submit timesheets immediately after each session.
  • Review monthly reports in your dashboard.
