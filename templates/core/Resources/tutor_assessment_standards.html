University Assessment & Marking Standards

Updated January 2026 · TASCAM Academic Advisors

Understanding how universities assess coursework enables tutors to provide accurate guidance that strengthens student performance without breaching academic integrity policies.

1. What Are Assessment Criteria?

Most UK and international universities grade work using criteria such as:

  • Argument quality
  • Use of evidence
  • Structure and clarity
  • Critical thinking
  • Methodological accuracy

2. Marking Rubrics

Tutors should guide students in understanding rubrics but never interpret them as an examiner.

3. Giving Feedback the Right Way

  • Comment on clarity—not rewrite paragraphs.
  • Highlight gaps—not fill them yourself.
  • Suggest improvements—not provide solutions.

4. Maintaining Academic Integrity

Feedback must never cross into authorship. The student must remain the sole creator of all submitted work.
